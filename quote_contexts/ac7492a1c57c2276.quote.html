4.1.1 FLOOR

The floor of the building was a dimly lit, drab area with concrete walls and a grey, checkered floor. The only light came from a few flickering fluorescent tubes overhead, casting an eerie glow over the space. The air was stale and musty, thick with the scent of old books and dampness. A row of worn, wooden benches lined one wall, facing a large, stone pedestal that supported a massive, leather-bound tome.
