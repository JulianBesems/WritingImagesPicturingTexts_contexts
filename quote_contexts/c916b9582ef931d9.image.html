Another attitude that we should discuss is the use of AI in this thesis. This is also immediately a way to say that in the work you will constantly find ways to contextualise what is presented, almost like a digital foldout page. Instead here the context is not a justification of what is there, it just shows the environment from which it was chosen and observed.
