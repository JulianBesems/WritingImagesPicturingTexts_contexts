4.1.2 WALL

The wall, a sturdy barrier, stood tall and proud, its rough stones bearing the scars of countless battles. The once-vibrant colors of the city's flags now faded, like the memories of its people. The silence was oppressive, heavy with the weight of forgotten dreams.
