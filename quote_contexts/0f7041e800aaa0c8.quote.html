The subtitles form the documents through which the textual word elements are defined. The subtitles are extracted from the .srt files as separate lines with timestamps. Then the subtitles across each film are combined as one large subtitle document, and after the same preprocessing steps used for the books are applied to all films, a vocabulary of the words used in the films is established. This vocabulary we will refer to as the mm_215_vocab_t .
