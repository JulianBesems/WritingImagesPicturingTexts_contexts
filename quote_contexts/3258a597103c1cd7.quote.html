Let's start with a definition on the ornament:

An ornament is a decorative object, often made of glass, metal, or ceramic, designed to be hung or placed on a Christmas tree, mantel, or other festive location. Its primary purpose is to add visual appeal and festive cheer to the holiday season. With its intricate designs and sparkling colors, an ornament can become a treasured family heirloom or a cherished keepsake.
