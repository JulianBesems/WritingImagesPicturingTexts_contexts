. . the floor. Thats right. The floor. Its typically the largest horizontal surface in the room and undeniably the most used. To provide a flat, horizontal surface on which desired human activities can take place, all buildings contain at least one floor. The walls are a large plane.  The sofa is a large plane.  The floor coverings and floor are large planes. ALL you need is a plane and a nice clear piece of wood. For example, a marble runner within a wooden floor could form a pathway through a hall and into a living space, perhaps continuing to the fireplace or window;

the fireplace or window, providing a natural visual focus for the room. The plane of the floor then becomes a unifying element, tying together the various planes of the walls, ceiling, and furniture. By incorporating plane elements in a harmonious manner, the overall aesthetic of the space can be greatly enhanced.
