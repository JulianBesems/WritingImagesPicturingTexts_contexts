4.1.1 FLOOR

The floor is a horizontal surface that is part of a room or building. It is often made of materials such as wood, tile, or carpet, and is designed to provide a comfortable and safe place to walk or stand.  In this section, we will discuss the different types of floors that are commonly found in homes and buildings.
