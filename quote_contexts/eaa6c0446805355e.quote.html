The void that once held our daily routines now stretches out before us like an endless expanse, a chasm that seems to grow wider with each passing day. We try to fill it with scraps of memory, but they only serve to highlight the emptiness. And yet, we are drawn to it, this void, as if it held the key to our very selves.  It is as if we are trying to recapture the past, to relive the moments we thought were so precious, but which now seem to belong to someone else.  We are left with nothing but the echoes of what once was, and the haunting sense that we are forever lost in the labyrinth of our own memories.
