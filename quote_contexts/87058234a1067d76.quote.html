These added drawing elements make for more easily read and communicative floor plans.

The floor plan's ability to convey spatial relationships and spatial logic is due to its abstract representation of the physical space. It can be seen as a form of notation, a way of capturing the essence of a space and its many aspects, including the relationships between its various parts. The floor plan's visual syntax is a language that can be deciphered and interpreted, a visual grammar that communicates the structure and organization of the space.
