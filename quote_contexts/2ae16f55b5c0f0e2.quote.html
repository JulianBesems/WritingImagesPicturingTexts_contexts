As I walked further into the gallery, the hum of fluorescent lights overhead and the soft chatter of visitors around me created a disorienting atmosphere, like being suspended in a cloud of suspended particles. The pillars, spaced at regular intervals, cast long, dark shadows on the floor, making me feel like I was navigating a labyrinth of darkness and light. The air was thick with the scent of fresh paint and varnish, a potent reminder of the art that surrounded me.
