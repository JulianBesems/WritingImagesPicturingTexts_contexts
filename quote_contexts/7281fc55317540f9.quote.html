4.1.1 FLOOR . . the floor. Thats right. The floor. Its typically the largest horizontal surface in the room and undeniably the most used. The floor is a site of the body in extremis. In tragic history plays and epics, at the point of climax, characters must keel over or fall over backwards onto the floor. As an element, what role does it play?

It is the threshold between the vertical and the horizontal, the boundary between the up and the down. It is the stage for the drama of life, where the characters of our daily routines play out their scenes. It is the place where we come to rest, to recharge, and to refuel for the next act.
