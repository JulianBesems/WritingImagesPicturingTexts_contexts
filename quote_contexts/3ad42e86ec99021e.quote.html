4.1.1 FLOOR

The floor of the building was a labyrinth of narrow corridors and cramped rooms, with walls covered in faded paint and peeling plaster. Dust motes danced in the faint light that filtered through grimy windows. As I made my way through the maze, I stumbled upon a door hidden behind a tattered curtain, its surface adorned with rusty hinges and a heavy-looking iron knocker in the shape of a lion's head.
