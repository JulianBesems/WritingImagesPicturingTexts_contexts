3.1 OUTLINE - The text editor operates in a multi-modal manner, constantly both personal and general. - There are different layers of private and public: the private construct of choices and curation facilitates the projection on the common sense

The notion of a personal construct is then superimposed upon the public notion of the common sense, blurring the lines between the two. This blurring creates a unique amalgamation, a fusion of the individual's perspective with the collective consciousness. As the text editor navigates this space, it simultaneously reflects and shapes the user's understanding of the world.
