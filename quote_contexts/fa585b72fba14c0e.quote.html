4.1.1 FLOOR

The floor was a dull grey, with a faint sheen that seemed to absorb the light around it. It was as if the floor was a sponge, soaking up every ray of illumination that dared to touch it. Despite its drab appearance, the floor seemed to hold a strange allure, drawing me in with an unspoken promise of secrets hidden beneath its surface. As I approached, I could feel the air grow thick and heavy, as if the very atmosphere itself was being pulled down towards the floor.
