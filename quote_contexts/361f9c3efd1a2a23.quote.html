3.2 DOMAINS Domains in this context are the fields of information between which the projections take place, as well as those that establish said projections. In the architectural communication, domains could be understood as the ichnographic,

the geometric, and the constructive, among others. These domains are interconnected, forming a complex network that allows for the exchange of information between them. This is achieved through the use of common languages and formats, which facilitate communication and collaboration among the different disciplines involved in the architectural project.
