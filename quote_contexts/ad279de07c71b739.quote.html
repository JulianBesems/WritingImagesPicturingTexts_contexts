Specifically, the work proposes an architectonic reading of the multimodal gap: the inherent misalignment between image and text that remains ingrained in machine learning embeddings. Rather than treating this gap as a technical flaw to be overcome, it is reframed as a conceptual opportunity. Drawing from Simondon’s notion of transduction, Foucault’s analysis of Magritte’s pipe, and the epistemic logic of architectural modalities, the multimodal gap is approached as a productive obstacle for speculation.
