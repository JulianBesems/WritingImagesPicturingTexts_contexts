The screen's function as a divider of space is also closely tied to its ability to filter light and shadow. In this way, the woven wall not only creates a physical separation but also manipulates the visual environment within the dwelling. By admitting and rejecting various intensities of light, it establishes a dynamic interplay between the interior and exterior worlds. The threshold quality of the wall, once again, is underscored by its role in mediating the transition between these two realms.
