4.1.2 WALL How the lapse of time affects ownership and usage of the wall. Hanging loosely, perhaps flapping, the woven screen generates- and, crucially, symbolizes - the division and organization of activities within the primitive dwelling. The woven "wall" is an enabler of civilization; the thick structural wall merely a defender of it.The hanging wall has an apparent descent compared to the foot wall. He argued that structure is there within the wall, but the importance of the wall is the visible spatial enclosure. As thin as possible: fantasy of the two-dimensional wall, sketched in 3D by SANAA for the Kunstlinie Almere, Netherlands. The meaning of the wall is just as diverse as the uses of vertical surface can be, but there are at least two essential functions: providing structure and dividing space. Practically, however, the two modes of action are always in some sort united.

The woven screen's function as a divider allows for the creation of distinct areas within the dwelling, each with its own specific purpose and character. As the inhabitants of the dwelling adapt to their surroundings, the wall's role evolves, and new meanings are assigned to its presence. In this way, the wall becomes an integral part of the dwelling's narrative, a constant and evolving presence that shapes the daily lives of its occupants.
