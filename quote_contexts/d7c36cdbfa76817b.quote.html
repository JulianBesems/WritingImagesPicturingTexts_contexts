The current version of the text editor facilitates a constant movement between text onto itself, as well as text onto imagery, with further granularity to show where these projections derive from through the reveal of films. The other two movements from imagery onto itself as well as imagery onto text are capable within the architecture of the editor, but have not been included in the active version, but are instead facilitated through standalone jupyter notebooks. After all, the primary activity the interactive editor is to facilitate is the *writing* of this thesis, however we will later visit how a *picturing* of a text can be explored through this instrument as well.
