Although ornamentation might have gone out of fashion a bit in the modern "stile", we also have to admit that people still love decoration, most notably the most intimate surface of a person is more and more subject to decoration: the skin.

This is particularly evident in the world of tattooing, where people use their bodies as a canvas to express themselves, tell stories, or simply to have a unique conversation starter. With the rise of tattooing's popularity, it's not uncommon for people to spend hours, even days, in a tattoo parlor, carefully designing a piece of art that will adorn their skin for the rest of their lives.
