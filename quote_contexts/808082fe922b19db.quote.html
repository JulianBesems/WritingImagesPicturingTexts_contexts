A certain kind of *unlimited* sits behind contemporary multimodal systems: oppositions and differences that are present everywhere, yet not necessarily brought into determinate relation. Something similar happens when a latent space becomes a general interior without thresholds. A full latent space being unlimited in what it can hold, but only navigatable if brought into a relationship with the input. In a way the state of latent space in ML, although fully governed by numbers, becomes without distance if abstracted away from the computational interaction. Everything becomes comparable and proportional to everything else. In heideggers words: “What is happening here when, as a result of the abolition of great distances, everything is equally far and equally near? What is this uniformity in which everydung is neither far nor near-is, as it were, without distance?” The ambition of the thesis, expressed through WIPTe is that the task is not to abolish that comparability, but to reintroduce distance as a practicable condition, so that one can actually enter the field rather than only sim it through similarity of iterated inputs.
