4.1.1 FLOOR navigation

The FLOOR navigation menu allows users to browse and access various functions and options within the system. It provides a hierarchical structure, enabling users to easily locate and access the desired features and settings. The menu is designed to be user-friendly, with clear and concise labels and icons to facilitate navigation.
