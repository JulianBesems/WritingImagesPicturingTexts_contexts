4.1.2 WALL

The walls of the ancient structure were worn and faded, the once-vibrant colors of the frescoes and murals now chipped and peeling away. Despite the passage of time, the intricate carvings and bas-reliefs remained remarkably well-preserved, telling the story of a long-forgotten civilization. As the team leader, Dr. Maria Rodriguez, carefully made her way through the dusty, cobweb-filled halls, she couldn't help but feel a sense of awe and wonder at the secrets that lay hidden within these ancient walls.
