The Writing Images and Picturing Text editor (WIPTe) forms the primary subject of this thesis, as well as its operation and context. The primary focus in constructing this multi-modal instrument together is to facilitate a constant setting within which this thesis is to be written. Therefore turning on itself both as an outline of what it is proposing as a research direction, all the time whilst demonstrating the outcome of its own question, simultaneaously. Therefore it was deemed of importance that WIPT was finished before the final writing stage. This chapter will go through all of the steps of its implementation, whilst continuing to be accompanied by the imagery and textual contexts it puts forward.
